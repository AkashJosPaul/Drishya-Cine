PRADEEP NAIR

Founder, Chairman and Managing Director
PRADEEP NAIR Founder, Chairman and Managing Director Mr. Pradeep Nair is the Founder, Chairman and MD of Drishya Cine Pvt. Ltd. In his capacity at the helm, he not only leads the company but is also an actor par excellence. He has seen immense success in a career spanning 30 years in Automobile and Construction Industry. He was the Chairman and MD of a Multinational Building Material manufacturing in Vietnam, prior to which he was the Sr Vice President of a Multinational Automotive Company. He was responsible for the launch for the Green protocol pre- engineered houses in India. Mr. Pradeep has started his acting career as the iconic mythological character "VAVAR" , in Ayyappa Sharnam serial, telecast on Amrita TV. He has an MBA in Marketing , and is a Mechanical engineering graduate. An ardent fan of cricket since childhood, he professionally represented his University and company, he worked with. His long cherished dream of performing arts has driven him to start this production and distribution Film company ..
